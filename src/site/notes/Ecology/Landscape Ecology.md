# Land Cover patterns and change
- Land Cover = physical material of earth at location
- Land Use = human use, such as "agriculture" or "urban"
# Patch-level metrics
- Area
- Shape:
	- Perimeter-area ratio
	- Others such as ratio to circle with area of circle of radius equal to distance across
# Landscape-level metrics
- patch-level metrics summarized at landscape level - statistics of all shapes, areas within a study area (landscape)
- Distance between patches, connectivity
